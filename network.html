Our Network



With 2 hubs and 3 focus airports in Italy's principal cities, Alitalia Virtual seamlessly connects travelers across the globe. We offer full coverage of our home market, as well as some of the only direct connections to Italy from many cities and countries worldwide



Hubs

Milano-Linate (LIML)

Linate is the smallest airport covering the Milan region, but it is the only one situated in the heart of the city, providing for quick and seamless connections from the airplane to the center of town (in contrast to Malpensa, which is 50 km from the city of Milan). This airport serves as Alitalia Virtual's secondary hub, with connections across Italy and Europe.

Rome-Fiumicino (LIRF)

Situated 35 km from the historic center of the Italian capital, the Aeroporto Internazionale di Roma-Fiumicino "Leonardo da Vinci" is one of Europe's busiest airports. This serves as the main hub for Alitalia Virtual, with an extensive network covering Italy and Europe, as well as connections to many intercontinental destinations.
