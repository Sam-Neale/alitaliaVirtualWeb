We are Alitalia Virtual


Moddled after Italy's global carrier


Alitalia Virtual is a virtual airline created for users of the Infinite Flight simulator. We strive to recreate the operations of Alitalia, Italy's flag carrier. We are not affiliated with the real Alitalia airline company, SkyTeam or any of their respective subsidiaries.
