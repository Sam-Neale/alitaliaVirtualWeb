About Us

Our Story


The new Alitalia Virtual was founded in June 2020 with the purpose of replicating the operations of Italy's leading airline group, Alitalia - Società Aerea Italiana, S.p.A., on the Infinite Flight platform. At AZVA, we offer a laid-back but professional environment, where our pilot members can improve their flying skills and have fun at the same time.

While realism is a central part of our daily operations, we have structured ourselves in such a way that allows us to use the full resources at our disposal. This means, for example, that our fleet includes certain aircraft types that are no longer operated by Alitalia in real life, but that are available in the Alitalia livery on Infinite Flight.

As new recruits, AZVA pilots will embark on a journey that will allow them to grow alongside us: starting as Trainees flying for our subsidiary Alitalia Cityliner Virtual, unlocking new aircraft models as they rise in seniority and earn new ranks, all the way to reaching the level of Alitalia Virtual Commander.
